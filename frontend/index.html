Help Center & FAQ

Everything you need to know about PetsBattleDApp

How to connect and start?

To use our DApp, you need the MetaMask extension. Make sure your network is set to Sepolia Testnet. You can get free test ETH from a Sepolia Faucet to pay for transaction gas.

What is PBT Token and how to get it?

PBT (Pet Battle Token) is our ecosystem's native token. You can see your balance in the top right corner. PBT is required for staking in the Arena and increases your pet's combat power based on your holdings.

How do Pet Campaigns work?

Users can launch "Crowdfunding" for new pets in the Services tab. Once a campaign reaches its ETH goal, the pet can be minted as a unique NFT. If you contribute to a campaign, you help bring a new pet to life!

Technical Stack & Security

Our DApp uses Ethers.js v6 for blockchain communication. Smart contracts are built using OpenZeppelin standards (ERC-20 for PBT and ERC-721 for Pets). All transactions are verifiable on the Sepolia Etherscan.
